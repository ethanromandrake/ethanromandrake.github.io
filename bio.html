About Me

But without ever forgetting to place the bright nickel-sized ball of hope in your palm. His medium slips in and out of a digital skin. Humor and Earnestly dance and tremble in terror. Ethan Roman Drake is confused. His video works freeze the train robbery. His songs ask what the truth is and support the opposite in the system of beliefs. Ethan Roman Drake knows there is a pattern that he cannot see.
